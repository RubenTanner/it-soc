About The IT Society

Your gateway to the world of technology and computing

The IT Society is an academic and social group aimed at supporting students who are taking a computing-based course or are interested in learning about IT.

We run sessions covering all disciplines, from coding to cyber security to networking - we have something for everyone. We meet every Thursday in the FTC building and have bi-weekly socials.
